History

Buckeye Molded Products Inc. was formed with the goal of utilizing over 150 years combined experience in the manufacturing of the highest quality electrical motor brushes. Our team of engineers and scientists work closely with our customers to develop the best solutions for their individual motor application and contact needs. We specialize in carbon, copper-graphite, silver-graphite, and custom made brushes and contacts. These all are manufactured in the most cost-effective manner to surpass customer requirements for performance and longevity.

Buckeye Molded Products is in conformance with ISO 9001:2000 Standard and has successfully implemented and Management System which has been registered by Alliance International Registrar, LLC.
